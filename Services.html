Services offered

We buy second hand Clothes

At Victorious Clothing, we believe in sustainable fashion and the importance of reducing waste. That's why we offer a unique service of buying second-hand clothes. If you have gently used clothing items that you no longer need, you can bring them to us, and we'll evaluate them for purchase.

we manufacture our own clothes

At Victorious Clothing, we take pride in designing and manufacturing our own line of high-quality garments. Our skilled team of designers, pattern makers, and tailors work meticulously to bring our unique vision to life. We ensure that every item meets our strict standards of craftsmanship and style.

Pricing: Starting from $50

we train small retaling businesses

At Victorious Clothing, we are dedicated to supporting small retailing businesses and helping them thrive in the competitive fashion industry. We offer comprehensive training programs designed to equip entrepreneurs with the knowledge and skills needed to succeed.

Pricing: Starting from $500

We sow torn clothes

At Victorious Clothing, we understand the sentiment attached to your favorite garments, even when they're torn or damaged. Our skilled seamstresses provide expert sewing services to mend and restore torn clothes, giving them a new lease on life.

Pricing: Starting from $15 per repair

We paint customer products

At Victorious Clothing, we offer a unique service of custom product painting, where we transform ordinary items into personalized pieces of art. Our talented artists specialize in painting on various surfaces, including clothing, accessories, and home decor items.

Pricing: Starting from $30 per item

Clothes Production

At Victorious Clothing, we take pride in our ability to design and manufacture high-quality clothes. With our dedicated team of designers, pattern makers, and skilled artisans, we offer comprehensive clothes production services to bring your fashion ideas to life.

Pricing: Starting from $20 per garment
